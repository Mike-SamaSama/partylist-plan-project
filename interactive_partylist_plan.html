A Step-by-Step Implementation Guide

This guide outlines the preparation required for a new organized group to establish itself and register as a Party-List Organization in the Philippines, targeting the 2028 National and Local Elections (NLE).

This interactive guide is based on R.A. No. 7941 and relevant COMELEC resolutions.
